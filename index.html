Privacy Policy

Our app is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal audio recordings, text inputs, or generated summaries to our servers.

1. On-Device Processing

All transcription (via Whisper) and summarization (via Gemma 4) occurs locally on your device. Your data never leaves your hardware.

2. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with Google, OpenAI, or any third-party analytics providers.
